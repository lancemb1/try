Top Excursions in Tamarindo, Costa Rica

  1. Scuba Diving
    • Lots of stringrays, lobsters, white tip reef sharks, pufferfish, eels, and many other species of animal life
  2. Waterfall Tour
    • Beautiful clear waterfall with hanging bridges through a damp forest
  3. Crocadile Tour
    • Massive American Crocadiles and Howler Monkeys
